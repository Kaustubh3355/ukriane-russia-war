Strategies used by both the countries

Ukraine

Ukraine has used a combination of military operations, indirect approach, and opportunism to exploit the weaknesses and incompetence of Russian forces. Ukraine also seeks to degrade Russia’s supply lines and artillery power by striking deep into occupied territories. Ukraine’s strategy is supported by the United States and aligned with European values

Ruusia

Russia’s invasion of Ukraine is the latest example of its long-standing strategy to expand its power. The West needs to understand this new kind of war. In his “declaration of war” speech to the nation on February 24, Putin set out the objectives of his “special operation”: his goals were to “strive for the demilitarization and denazification of Ukraine”.
